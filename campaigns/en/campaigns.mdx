Campaigns let you send proactive WhatsApp messages to a list of recipients using pre-approved message templates. You can send them immediately or schedule them for a specific date and time—all from within the Semantiks Console.

This is useful for:
- Announcing promotions, events, or product launches
- Sending reminders or follow-ups to customer segments
- Re-engaging users who haven't interacted recently

---

## Campaigns table

When you open **Proactive AI > Engagement > Campaigns**, you see a table listing all campaigns (proactive messages) created for the current agent. Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Template** | The WhatsApp message template used |
| **Channel** | The channel the campaign was sent through |
| **Scheduled** | The date and time the campaign is scheduled to be sent |
| **Recipients** | How many phone numbers were included |
| **Status** | Current delivery status (see below) |
| **Actions** | Options to duplicate or delete the campaign |

Use the **search bar** to filter campaigns by template name, and the **status filter** to show only campaigns with a specific status.

---

## Campaign statuses

Each campaign displays a color-coded status badge:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Scheduled** | The campaign is queued and will be sent at the configured date and time |
| **Sent** | The messages have been dispatched to Meta's API |
| **Delivered** | All messages have been confirmed as delivered or read by recipients |
| **Failed** | The campaign could not be delivered |

<Note>
The platform refines the status automatically as Meta sends delivery webhooks. A campaign initially marked **Sent** may update to **Delivered** once all recipients confirm receipt.
</Note>

For campaigns that are not in a **Failed** state and have delivery tracking data, the badge also displays a **delivered/total** counter (e.g. `12/15`) so you can see progress at a glance.

When a campaign has **Failed**, the badge includes a warning icon. Hover over it to see a tooltip with the failure reason.

### Common delivery errors

| Error | Description |
|---|---|
| **Unsettled payments** | There are pending payments in Meta — check your WhatsApp Business account billing |
| **24-hour window expired** | More than 24 hours have passed since the customer's last reply |
| **Undeliverable number** | The phone number is not reachable on WhatsApp |

## Delivery detail dialog

Click on any campaign's **status badge** to open the **Delivery detail** dialog. This dialog shows a per-recipient breakdown of the campaign's delivery results.

For each recipient you'll see:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Phone number** | The number the message was sent to |
| **Delivery status** | One of: **Read**, **Delivered**, **Failed**, or **No confirmation** |
| **Error detail** | If delivery failed, a human-readable explanation of the error is shown below the phone number |

<Tip>
Use the delivery detail dialog to quickly identify which specific recipients had delivery issues and take action—for example, verifying phone numbers or checking your Meta billing status.
</Tip>

<Note>
For campaigns that were scheduled or sent before delivery tracking was enabled, the dialog will show a message indicating that no per-recipient records are available.
</Note>

## Creating a campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the campaign form">
    Click **Create Campaign** at the top of the Campaigns page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a template">
    Choose a pre-approved WhatsApp message template. Only templates that have been approved by Meta are available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your recipients">
    Add the phone numbers you want to send the message to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule or send immediately">
    Choose a date and time for delivery, or send the campaign right away.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm and send">
    Review your campaign details and click **Send** to queue it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Managing campaigns

From the campaigns table you can:
- **Duplicate** a campaign to quickly create a new one with the same template and settings
- **Delete** a campaign you no longer need

<Warning>
Deleting a campaign removes it permanently. Scheduled campaigns that haven't been sent yet will be cancelled.
</Warning>
