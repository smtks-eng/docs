This guide will walk you through integrating your Semantiks AI agent with WhatsApp Business Manager.

## 1. Create a WhatsApp App
- Log in to your [Facebook Business Manager](https://business.facebook.com/).
- Go to **Business Settings > WhatsApp Accounts**.
- Click **Add** to create a new WhatsApp app/account.

## 2. Retrieve API Keys
- In your WhatsApp app settings, navigate to **API Keys** or **Access Tokens**.
- Copy the required keys (e.g., `WhatsApp Business API Key`).

## 3. Configure Callback URLs
- In the WhatsApp app settings, find the **Webhook** or **Callback URL** section.
- Enter the callback URL provided by Semantiks (e.g., `https://your-semantiks-endpoint.com/webhook/whatsapp`).
- Save your changes.

## 4. Finalize Integration in Semantiks
- Go to your Semantiks Console and open the WhatsApp integration section.
- Paste your API keys and confirm the callback URL.
- Test the connection to ensure everything is working.

<Note>
If you need more help, contact Semantiks support or refer to the WhatsApp Business API documentation.
</Note>
