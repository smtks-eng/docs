This guide walks you through connecting your AI agent to **WhatsApp Business** using the Meta (Facebook) integration.

## Prerequisites

- A **Meta Business** account.
- A **phone number** that is not already registered with another WhatsApp Business API provider.
- Admin access to the Semantiks Console.

---

## Step-by-step setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the WhatsApp channel card">
    In your agent, go to **Connect > Channels** and click the **WhatsApp** card under the Meta section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the Meta connection flow">
    Click **Connect** and follow the Meta authentication prompts. You will be asked to log in to your Meta Business account and grant permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your phone number">
    Choose the phone number you want to use for WhatsApp Business. If your number is new, Meta will guide you through adding it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete registration">
    After connecting, check the **registration status** badge next to the channel name:
    - If the status is **Verification needed**, click the **Verify** button to complete phone number verification with Meta. You can receive a 6-digit code via **SMS** or **Voice call**, then enter it to confirm ownership. See the [Connect page docs](/agent/en/channels) for detailed steps.
    - Once verified (or if verification is not required), click the **Register** button, enter a **6-digit PIN**, and submit the registration to Meta.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the number is registered">
    The status badge should update to **Registered**. Your agent is now live on WhatsApp.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Remember the 6-digit PIN you set during registration — you will need it if you ever re-register the same number.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  After connecting, you can manage message templates for WhatsApp. See [How to Manage Message Templates](/integration-guides/en/how-to-manage-message-templates-whatsapp) for details.
</Tip>
