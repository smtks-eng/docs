## Setting up with Semantiks
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Semantiks-Console and create an account">
    Open [Semantiks-Console](https://consola.semantiks.com/login) log-in and [create](https://consola.semantiks.com/signup) an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up with your preferred method">
    You can Sign Up using Google or Github or choose your own password with your email. After filling the necessary information you are going to recibe the corresponding notification via email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    You will receive an email for account verification. After verifying, you will be redirected to start the creation of your agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill your account information">
    First, fill your information for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your organization information">
    Fill the Organization information.

    <Note>
      If you already have an account created and associated to an organization please go directly to the ORGANIZATIONS section of the doc where you can find how to add users to an existing organization
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an agent type">
    After filling the **organization information** select an agent type

    <Note>
      If you want to know more about the **agent types** please see the corresponding data in the Agents section.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the agent identity">
    Set the agent identity by filling the form with the agent name, description, and other details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
