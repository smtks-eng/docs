## Setting up with Semantiks
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Semantiks-Console and create an account">
    Open [Semantiks-Console](https://consola.semantiks.com/login) log-in and [create](https://consola.semantiks.com/signup) an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up with your preferred method">
    You can Sign Up using Google or Github or choose your own password with your email. After filling the necessary information you are going to recibe the corresponding notification via email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    You will receive an email for account verification. After verifying, you may be placed on a **waiting list** while your account is reviewed. During this time you will see a verification page confirming that your account is under review.

    <Note>
      The verification page supports **language switching** — use the toggle in the top-right corner to switch between English and Spanish. You will receive a confirmation email within up to 2 business days.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill your account information">
    First, fill your information for your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your organization information">
    Fill the Organization information. When selecting your **country**, you will see priority countries (Mexico, Argentina, Brazil, Colombia, Peru) listed at the top for quick access, followed by the rest of the countries in alphabetical order.

    <Note>
      If you already have an account created and associated to an organization please go directly to the ORGANIZATIONS section of the doc where you can find how to add users to an existing organization
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your agent">
    After filling the **organization information**, create your agent and continue with the initial setup flow.

    - **Agent type**: Choose the type of agent you want to create by clicking one of the available options: **Pre-sales**, **Customer Service**, or **Reactivation**. The selected type will be highlighted. Based on your selection, the agent's goal will be pre-filled with a suggested description.
    - **Agent name**: Give your agent a recognizable name.
    - **Agent goal**: Review and optionally edit the pre-filled goal for your agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the agent identity">
    Set the agent identity by filling the form with the agent name, description, and other details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
