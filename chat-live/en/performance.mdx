title: "Performance"
description: "Track your live-chat team's throughput, response times, and CSAT with leaderboards and time-series charts."
---

The **Performance** page gives supervisors and managers a data-driven view of how the human support team is performing on escalated conversations. Use it to spot trends, compare specialists, and drill into the conversations behind the numbers.

## Date range & granularity

At the top of the page you can choose:

- **Preset period** — Last 24 hours, Last 7 days, Last 30 days, or Last 90 days.
- **Granularity** — Daily, Weekly, or Monthly buckets for the throughput chart.

All cards, charts, and the leaderboard update to reflect the selected period.

## Headline metrics

Four KPI cards summarize the period at a glance:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total handled** | Number of escalated conversations attended by at least one specialist |
| **Resolved** | Number of those conversations that were closed/resolved |
| **Avg first response** | Average time from escalation to the specialist's first reply |
| **Avg CSAT** | Average customer-satisfaction score across rated conversations |

## Throughput chart

A time-series bar chart shows **handled** vs **resolved** conversations per bucket (day, week, or month). Hover over any bar to see exact counts.

## Channel mix

A breakdown of escalated conversations by channel (e.g., WhatsApp, Freshchat, Web) so you can see where your team's effort is concentrated.

## Leaderboard

The leaderboard ranks every specialist who handled conversations in the selected period. Columns include:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **#** | Rank |
| **Specialist** | Name and avatar |
| **Handled** | Conversations attended |
| **Avg first response** | Average time to first reply |
| **Avg resolution** | Average time to close a conversation |
| **Avg CSAT** | Average satisfaction score |

### Viewing a specialist's conversations

Click any row in the leaderboard to open a **side panel** that lists all escalated conversations attended by that specialist during the selected period. Each card in the panel shows:

- **Topic** — the auto-classified conversation topic (or "No topic" if none was detected).
- **Status** — **Open** (amber badge) or **Resolved** (green badge).
- **Date & time** — when the conversation was created.
- **CSAT** — the satisfaction score if one was collected.

At the top of the panel you'll see the total conversation count and a **View all on the board** button.

<Tip>
Click any individual conversation card to jump directly to the **Conversations Explorer** filtered to that single conversation. Use the **View all on the board** button to open the Explorer pre-filtered to *all* of that specialist's conversations.
</Tip>

When you navigate to the Conversations Explorer from this panel, a **pinned chip** appears in the filter bar showing the number of linked conversations and the specialist's name. Remove the chip to return to the normal, unfiltered view of all conversations.

## Common workflows

- **Identify coaching opportunities** — sort the leaderboard by Avg CSAT or Avg first response to find specialists who may benefit from additional training.
- **Investigate a spike** — if the throughput chart shows an unusual peak, click the corresponding specialists in the leaderboard to read the actual conversations.
- **Verify resolution quality** — open the side panel for a specialist and review their resolved conversations to ensure they meet quality standards.
