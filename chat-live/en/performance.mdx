title: "Performance"
description: "Monitor your live chat team's throughput, SLA compliance, channel distribution, and individual agent performance."
---

The **Performance** dashboard gives live-chat supervisors a comprehensive view of how the human support team is handling escalated conversations. Use it to track daily volume, compare individual agent output, review SLA targets, and understand channel distribution—all from a single page.

## Date range

At the top of the page you can select a preset period (**Today**, **7 D**, **30 D**, **Year**) or pick a custom range with the date picker. Every chart and metric on the page updates to reflect the chosen window.

---

## Headline KPIs

Four cards summarize overall performance for the selected period:

| Card | Description |
|---|---|
| **Handled** | Total conversations handled by the team |
| **Avg first response** | Average time from conversation assignment to the specialist's first reply |
| **Avg resolution** | Average time from first message to conversation close |
| **Avg CSAT** | Average customer-satisfaction score (when collected) |

---

## Throughput per day

A bar chart showing daily conversation volume. A **specialist filter** dropdown next to the section title lets you choose whose data to display:

- **All specialists** (default) — stacked bars split into **Resolved** (closed) and **Open** conversations, with a color legend at the bottom of the chart.
- **A specific specialist** — a single-color bar showing that member's daily handled count, so you can evaluate individual output against a clean baseline.

The dropdown lists every specialist that appears in the leaderboard for the selected date range.

<Tip>
Switch to a specific specialist to quickly see their daily workload pattern—useful during 1-on-1 performance reviews.
</Tip>

---

## SLA status

Progress bars showing compliance against SLA targets:

| Metric | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **On time** | Percentage of conversations where first response met the SLA target |
| **At risk** | Conversations approaching the SLA threshold |
| **Breached** | Conversations that exceeded the SLA target |

<Warning>
SLA targets are currently **placeholders** — they are not yet wired to real data. The values shown are illustrative only.
</Warning>

---

## Channel mix

A donut chart with a breakdown legend showing how conversations are distributed across your connected channels. Each segment represents a channel, and the legend displays the percentage share.

---

## Agent leaderboard

A ranked table of specialists sorted by number of conversations handled (descending). Each row shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Agent** | Specialist name with a color-coded avatar. The avatar color matches the color used in the throughput chart when that specialist is selected. |
| **Handled** | Total conversations handled |
| **Resolved** | Total conversations closed/resolved |
| **Avg first response** | Average first-response time |
| **CSAT** | Average customer satisfaction score |

Click any row to open a **side panel** with more detail about that specialist's conversations.

### Specialist side panel

The panel displays:

- **Summary stats** — handled, resolved, first-response time, and CSAT for the selected specialist.
- **Conversation list** — every conversation the specialist participated in during the period. Each card shows the conversation topic (or "No topic" if unclassified), a short conversation ID snippet, and the conversation status (**Resolved** or **Open**). Click a card to navigate to the full conversation in the Conversations view.

<Tip>
Use the specialist panel to audit individual agent quality—review topics, resolution rates, and jump into specific conversations that need attention.
</Tip>

---

## Common workflows

- **Daily standup** — set the range to *Today* or *7 D* and review headline KPIs plus the leaderboard to identify who may need support.
- **Capacity planning** — use the throughput chart with *All specialists* to spot volume trends, then switch to individual specialists to see if workload is evenly distributed.
- **Channel strategy** — check the channel mix to understand which channels drive the most human-handled volume and allocate resources accordingly.
