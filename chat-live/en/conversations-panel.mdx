The **Conversations Panel** is your central workspace for handling live customer conversations. From here, you can view active and past conversations, read transcripts in real time, and access a detailed sidebar with customer information and agent tools.

---

## Panel layout

The Live Chat view is split into three main areas:

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **Conversation list** | A sidebar on the left showing all conversations (active, queued, ended). Use the search and filter controls to find specific sessions. |
| **Chat transcript** | The central area displays the full message thread of the selected conversation in real time. |
| **Customer details panel** | A collapsible panel on the right with contact information, conversation metadata, labels, internal notes, and more. |

---

## Customer Details Panel

Click any conversation to open the **Customer Details Panel** on the right side. This panel gives you context about the user and the current session without leaving the chat view.

The panel includes the following sections:

### Contact Info
Displays available contact details such as the user's name, phone number, external ID, and the channel the conversation originated from.

### Activity
Shows timestamps for when the contact was created and when they were last active.

### Labels

The **Labels** section lets you attach color-coded tags to any conversation. Labels are a flexible way to categorize, prioritize, or annotate conversations so your team can spot patterns at a glance—both here and in the [Conversations dashboard](/dashboards/en/conversations).

#### Adding a label

1. In the Customer Details panel, find the **Labels** section (identified by a tag icon).
2. Click the **+ Add** button. A popover opens with a name field and a color picker.
3. Type a short name for the label (up to 22 characters). Names are displayed in uppercase.
4. Choose a color using any of the available methods:
   - **Color square** — click or drag on the saturation/brightness area.
   - **Hue slider** — slide to pick the base hue.
   - **Hex input** — type a hex code directly.
   - **Swatches** — click a preset color.
   - **Eye dropper** — pick a color from anywhere on your screen (supported browsers only).
5. Click **Add** to save the label.

#### Editing a label

Click the **pencil icon** on any existing label chip. The same popover opens pre-filled with the label's current name and color so you can adjust either one. Click **Save** when done.

#### Removing a label

Click the **✕ icon** on the label chip to remove it immediately.

<Tip>
  Labels are saved per conversation. They also appear as a read-only **Labels** column in the Conversations Explorer table on the Conversations dashboard.
</Tip>

### Internal Notes
Add free-text notes to the conversation. These are visible only to your team and are never shown to the end user.

- Click **Add note** to create a new note.
- Each note shows the author and timestamp.
- You can edit or delete existing notes.

### Automations
If any automations ran during this conversation, they appear here with their execution status.

## Managing conversations

From the conversation list you can:

- **Search** by user name or external ID.
- **Filter** by status (active, ended, queued) or by channel.
- **End** a conversation manually when the interaction is complete.

<Note>
  Ending a conversation removes it from the active queue but keeps its full history accessible in the Conversations dashboard.
</Note>
