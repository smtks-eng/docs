title: "Conversations Panel"
description: "Manage live conversations with your contacts in real time. Take control from the AI, reply to users, and collaborate with your team."
---

The **Conversations Panel** is your team's real-time workspace for handling live conversations that have been escalated from the AI agent or that require human intervention.

From here you can monitor ongoing conversations, take control when needed, send replies, and hand conversations back to the AI.

## Viewing conversations

The left sidebar lists all active and recent conversations. Each entry shows:
- The **contact name** and **channel** (WhatsApp, Web, Freshchat, etc.)
- A preview of the **last message**
- The **status** of the conversation (active, ended, waiting, etc.)
- The **assigned member**, if someone has taken the conversation

Click any conversation to open it in the main panel.

## Taking a conversation

When a conversation is escalated or you want to intervene, click the **Take** button to assign it to yourself. This switches the conversation to **human-controlled** mode, meaning the AI agent will stop responding automatically.

- **Owners** and **Admins** can take any conversation, even if it's already assigned to another member.
- **Members** can take unassigned conversations. If a conversation is already assigned to someone else, members cannot take it—but they can still reassign it to another team member (excluding themselves).

## Replying to a conversation

Once a conversation is in human-controlled mode, the chat input becomes available **only to the team member who is assigned to it**. Owners and admins can also reply to any human-controlled conversation, regardless of assignment.

<Warning>
If you are not assigned to a conversation and you are not an owner or admin, the reply input will be disabled. You must take or be assigned the conversation before you can send messages.
</Warning>

Type your message and press **Send** to reply to the user directly.

### Notes mode

Toggle **Notes mode** to write internal notes that are visible only to your team—these messages are never sent to the contact.

## Reassigning a conversation

You can reassign a conversation to another team member using the assignment menu. This is useful for routing conversations to specialists or handing off between shifts.

- **Owners** and **Admins** can reassign any conversation to any member.
- **Members** can reassign conversations to other team members but cannot assign a conversation to themselves unless it is unassigned.

## Handing back to the AI

When you're done, click **Hand back to AI** (or the equivalent action) to return control to the AI agent. The agent will resume responding to the user automatically.

## Ending a conversation

You can manually end a conversation when the issue is resolved. Ended conversations move to the history and are no longer active.

<Tip>
Use the **Conversations Panel** together with the **Performance** and **Escalation Insights** pages to track your team's response quality and identify patterns in escalations.
</Tip>
