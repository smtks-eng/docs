description: "Manage live conversations with users across all channels from a unified inbox."
---

The **Conversations Panel** is the central workspace for live chat operators. It provides a real-time view of all active and recent conversations, letting your team monitor, join, and manage user interactions across every connected channel.

## Inbox Overview

The inbox displays a list of conversations on the left and a chat view on the right. Each conversation entry shows:

- **User name** (if available from the channel)
- **Channel icon** — indicating where the conversation is happening
- **Last message preview**
- **Timestamp** of the most recent message
- **Assignment status** — who is currently handling the conversation

## Filters

Use the filter bar above the conversation list to narrow down what you see:

- **Status** — Filter by conversation status (e.g., Active, Ended). Multi-select.
- **Channel** — Filter by communication channel (e.g., WhatsApp, Web Bubble, Freshchat). Single-select.
- **Assignment** — Filter by assignment type: conversations handled by AI, escalated to humans, assigned to you, or assigned to a specific team member. Multi-select.

<Tip>
**Your filters are remembered.** The platform automatically saves your inbox filter selections per agent. When you return to the Live Chat inbox, your previous filter choices (status, channel, and assignment) are restored so you can pick up right where you left off. This is especially useful for operators who consistently work with the same filter view.
</Tip>

## Conversation View

Click any conversation to open the full chat view. From here you can:

- **Read the full transcript** between the user and the AI agent (and any human operators)
- **Send messages** directly to the user
- **View conversation details** — status, channel, timestamps, response metrics, quality flag, and metadata
- **Assign or reassign** the conversation to another team member

## Key Notes

- The inbox updates in real time — new messages and conversations appear automatically.
- Conversations are scoped to the currently selected agent.
- The assignment filter defaults to showing conversations assigned to you, so you see your workload first.
