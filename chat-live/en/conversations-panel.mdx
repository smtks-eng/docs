The **Conversations Panel** is the main workspace for your live chat operation. It displays all conversations that have been escalated from the AI agent to human agents, allowing your team to monitor and respond in real time.

---

## Conversation List

The left sidebar shows a scrollable list of conversations. Each conversation item displays:

| Element | Description |
|---|---|
| **Avatar** | The contact's avatar with a small channel-glyph badge (e.g. WhatsApp, Web) |
| **Contact name** | The user's name (if known) or their external ID |
| **Last message preview** | A truncated preview of the most recent message |
| **Timestamp** | When the last message was sent |
| **Preassigned badge** | An amber "Take" badge appears on conversations that are preassigned and waiting for a human agent to claim them |

### Visual indicators

- **Active conversations** are highlighted with a **pink left border** so you can quickly spot them in the list.
- The **selected conversation** has a highlighted background to indicate it is currently open in the main panel.

<Tip>
  Use the filters at the top of the conversation list to narrow down by status, channel, or assigned agent.
</Tip>

---

## Conversation Detail

Click any conversation in the list to open it in the main area. From here you can:

- Read the full message history between the user and the agent(s)
- Send replies as a human agent
- View contact details and metadata in the right sidebar
- See which channel the conversation is happening on

---

## Filters

Use the filter controls at the top of the conversation list to find specific conversations:

- **Status** — filter by conversation status (active, closed, etc.)
- **Channel** — show only conversations from a specific channel
- **Assigned agent** — filter by the human agent handling the conversation

---

## Key notes

- Conversations appear in the panel as soon as they are escalated from the AI agent or created directly by a human agent.
- The panel updates in **real time** — new messages and status changes appear instantly.
- You can handle multiple conversations simultaneously by switching between them in the list.
