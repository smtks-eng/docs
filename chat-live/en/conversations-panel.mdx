The **Conversations Panel** is the central hub for your team's live chat operations. From here, team members can monitor active conversations, take over from the AI agent, reply to users, and manage escalated interactions.

## Panel overview

The Conversations Panel is divided into three areas:

| Area | Description |
|---|---|
| **Conversation list** (left) | Shows all conversations with filters for status, channel, and assignment. |
| **Chat view** (center) | The active conversation thread where you read messages and reply. |
| **Conversation detail** (right) | Contact info, conversation metadata, and actions like assign, transfer, or close. |

---

## Taking over a conversation

When the AI agent escalates a conversation or you want to intervene manually, you can **take over** the conversation. This puts the conversation into human-controlled mode, meaning the AI agent stops responding and a team member handles replies.

- Click the **Take conversation** button in the conversation detail panel.
- Once taken, the conversation is assigned to you and you can start typing replies.

---

## Replying to users

Only the team member who is **assigned to the conversation** can type replies. Owners and admins can also reply to any conversation regardless of assignment.

If you are not the assigned member and do not have owner/admin privileges, the reply area will be disabled.

---

## Re-engaging contacts (Start New)

You can re-engage a contact by starting a new conversation or sending a WhatsApp template message. This is useful for following up with users after a previous conversation has ended.

<Note>
The ability to start a new conversation or re-engage a contact is restricted to the **assigned team member**, or users with **owner** or **admin** roles. If the conversation is not assigned to you and you are not an owner or admin, you will see a warning message instead of the re-engage controls.
</Note>

---

## Transferring conversations

You can transfer a conversation to another team member if someone else is better suited to handle the request. Use the **transfer** option in the conversation detail panel.

---

## Closing conversations

Once a conversation is resolved, you can close it manually. Closed conversations move out of the active list but remain accessible in conversation history.

---

## Key permissions

| Action | Who can do it |
|---|---|
| **Take a conversation** | Any available team member |
| **Reply** | Assigned member, owner, or admin |
| **Start new / Re-engage** | Assigned member, owner, or admin |
| **Transfer** | Assigned member, owner, or admin |
| **Close** | Assigned member, owner, or admin |
