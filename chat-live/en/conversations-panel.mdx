The **Conversations Panel** is the main workspace for your Contact Center team. It shows every active and recent conversation across all connected channels, and lets agents take over from the AI, reply to users, and manage assignments—all from one place.

## Conversation list

The left side of the panel shows a scrollable list of conversations. Each entry displays:

- **Contact name** (or external ID if no name is available)
- **Channel** the conversation is on
- **Last message preview**
- **Status badge** — *Active*, *Ended*, or *Pre-assigned*
- **Handled by** — whether the conversation is currently managed by the AI agent or a human team member

Click any conversation to open its detail view on the right.

## Conversation detail

The right side shows the full message thread and conversation controls:

- **Message history** — the complete transcript between the user and the agent (AI or human)
- **Conversation metadata** — channel, status, assigned member, timestamps
- **Reply input** — type and send messages when the conversation is under human control

## Assigning & taking conversations

Conversations can be **assigned** to a specific team member. Assignment can happen automatically (e.g., via the Semantiks Contact Centre tool) or manually from the conversation detail.

### Pre-assigned conversations

A conversation is **pre-assigned** when it has an `assigned_to_member_id` set but `handled_by` is still `"agent"` — meaning the AI keeps replying until a human takes over. Pre-assigned conversations show an amber badge in the conversation detail.

### Taking a conversation

To take over a pre-assigned conversation (flipping `handled_by` from AI to human so the LLM stops replying), click the **Take conversation** button that appears in the conversation header.

<Note>
**Permission rules for taking conversations:**
- **Owners and Admins** can take any pre-assigned conversation, regardless of who it is assigned to.
- **Other team members** can only take conversations that are assigned to them. If a conversation is assigned to a different member, the Take button will not appear for you.
</Note>

### Sending messages on assigned conversations

The same permission rules apply when starting or re-engaging a conversation (e.g., sending a WhatsApp template or opening a new thread):

- **Owners and Admins** can send messages on any assigned conversation.
- **Other members** can only send messages on conversations assigned to them. If the conversation is assigned to someone else, the message input area is replaced by a notice: *"This conversation is assigned to another member."*

<Tip>
If you see the "assigned to another member" notice and believe you should handle the conversation, ask an **Owner** or **Admin** on your team to reassign it to you.
</Tip>

## Filters

Use the filter controls at the top of the conversation list to narrow down what you see:

- **Status** — active, ended
- **Channel** — filter by a specific channel
- **Assigned to** — show only conversations assigned to a specific member

## Key notes

- The Conversations Panel updates in **real time** — new messages and status changes appear automatically.
- A conversation under **human control** will not receive AI-generated replies until it is released back to the agent.
- Pre-assigned conversations let the AI continue responding while a human is designated, ensuring no user message goes unanswered.
