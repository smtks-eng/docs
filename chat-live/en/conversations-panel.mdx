title: "Conversations Panel"
description: "Manage and respond to live chat conversations escalated from your AI agent to human support."
---

The **Conversations Panel** is the main workspace for your support team when handling live conversations. It shows every conversation that has been escalated from the AI agent to a human, and lets your team take, reply, reassign, and resolve them in real time.

## Conversation list

On the left side of the panel you'll see the list of active conversations. Each entry shows:

- **Contact name** and **channel** (e.g. WhatsApp, Web Bubble)
- **Last message preview**
- **Assignment status** — whether the conversation is unassigned, pre-assigned, or being handled by a specific team member

Use the filters and search bar at the top to narrow the list by status, channel, or assignee.

## Taking & assigning conversations

When a conversation is escalated, it appears as **unassigned** (loose) in the panel. Team members can pick it up or pass it to a colleague.

### Who can take a conversation

| Scenario | Member | Admin / Owner |
|---|---|---|
| Conversation is **unassigned** | ✅ Can take | ✅ Can take |
| Conversation is **assigned to you** | ✅ Can take back | ✅ Can take back |
| Conversation is **assigned to someone else** | ❌ Cannot take | ✅ Can take |

- **Members** can only take conversations that are unassigned or already assigned to themselves.
- **Admins** and **Owners** can take any conversation, including ones currently assigned to another team member. This is useful for supervisors who need to step in, redistribute workload, or handle escalations directly.

### Passing conversations

Any team member—regardless of role—can **pass** (reassign) a conversation to another available member. When passing, the member list shows all available colleagues. If you cannot take the conversation yourself (because it is assigned to someone else and you are not an admin/owner), you will not appear in the assignee list.

### Pre-assignment

A conversation can be **pre-assigned** to a member while the AI agent is still handling it. In this state the `handled_by` field remains `agent` but the `assigned_to` is already set. The AI continues replying until the human explicitly takes over.

## Conversation detail

Click any conversation to open the detail view on the right. Here you can:

- **Read the full message history** between the contact and the agent/human
- **Send replies** as a human agent
- **View contact info** — name, channel, external ID, and collected memories
- **Reassign or resolve** the conversation using the action buttons

## Key notes

- Conversations stay in the panel until they are **resolved** (closed).
- Admins and owners have broader assignment privileges to support team supervision and workload management.
- The panel updates in **real time** — new messages and status changes appear instantly.

<Tip>
Use the **Performance** and **Escalation Insights** pages alongside the Conversations Panel to monitor your team's response times and identify patterns in escalated topics.
</Tip>
