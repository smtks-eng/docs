The **Billing** page gives you a clear view of your current subscription for the selected agent. You can review your plan, track usage costs, and download invoices—all in one place.

## Subscription overview

When you open Billing, you'll see a summary card for your active contract:

- **Plan name** — the rate card or contract name associated with this agent.
- **Status badge** — shows whether the subscription is active.
- **Total usage cost** — accumulated cost for the current billing cycle.
- **Billing period** — start and end dates of the current cycle.

## Invoices

Below the summary you'll find a list of invoices for the contract. Expand any invoice to see its details, then click **Download invoice** to get a PDF.

## Cancel a subscription

If you need to cancel your agent's subscription, click the **Cancel subscription** button next to the active status badge.

<Warning>
  Cancelling a subscription cannot be undone. The contract will end at the close of the current billing cycle and the agent will be disabled. After cancellation you can delete the agent.
</Warning>

### How it works

1. Click **Cancel subscription** on the subscription card.
2. A confirmation dialog will appear explaining what happens next.
3. Click **Yes, cancel subscription** to confirm, or **Keep subscription** to go back.
4. Once confirmed, the contract is scheduled to end at the close of the current cycle and the agent status changes to **disabled**.

### Usage commitment restriction

If your contract includes an active usage commitment, the **Cancel subscription** button will be disabled. You'll see a tooltip explaining:

> "You can't cancel while there is an active usage commitment."

You must wait until the usage commitment period ends before you can cancel.

<Note>
  If you need to cancel a subscription with an active commitment, please contact support.
</Note>
