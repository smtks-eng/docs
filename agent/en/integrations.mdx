**Integrations** connect your AI agent to external platforms and services. This is how you link your agent to messaging apps, customer support tools, CRMs, and other systems your team already uses.

Integrations operate at two levels:
- **Organization integrations** — connected once and shared across all agents in your organization
- **Channel integrations** — specific configurations that apply per agent and per channel

## Accessing Integrations

Navigate to **AI Agent > Connect > Integrations** in the left sidebar. You'll see all available integrations organized by type.

## Available integration types

### Messaging channels
Connect your agent to the platforms where your users are:
- **WhatsApp (Meta)** — Deploy your agent on WhatsApp Business via the Meta API
- **Freshchat** — Integrate with Freshchat for live chat support workflows
- **Zendesk** — Connect to Zendesk for ticketing and live chat support

### Web channels
- **Web Bubble** — Embed a chat widget on your website
- **External Chat URL** — Share a standalone chat link without embedding

<Tip>
For step-by-step setup instructions for each channel, see the **Integration Guides** section at the bottom of the left sidebar.
</Tip>

## Setting up an integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select an integration">
    Click on the integration you want to set up. You'll see the required credentials and configuration options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Provide the API keys, tokens, or authentication details required by the platform. These are encrypted and stored securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure channels">
    Once the integration is connected, configure the specific channels (e.g. a WhatsApp phone number, a Freshchat inbox) you want your agent to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign to your agent">
    In the **Channels** section of the Connect tab, assign the configured channel to your agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the connection">
    Use the **Playground** or send a test message through the channel to verify the integration is working correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key notes

- Integrations are **organization-level** — multiple agents in your organization can use the same connected integration.
- **Credentials are encrypted** — API keys and tokens are stored securely and never exposed in plain text.
- Some skills (like **Human Escalation**) only appear when a compatible integration (Freshchat or Zendesk) is connected.
- Disconnecting an integration will affect **all agents** that use it — proceed with care.
