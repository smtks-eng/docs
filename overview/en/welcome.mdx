## Your Console

The Semantiks Console is your operations center for creating and managing AI agents. Through this intuitive interface, you can:

- Design and customize your AI agents  
- Define your business knowledge  
- Deploy agents across multiple channels  
- Monitor performance and conversations  
- Schedule outreach and reactivation campaigns.

## Use cases you can cover

Semantiks AI lets you configure agents for different business goals using the same core product. Common examples include:

- Customer support and service
- Lead qualification and sales assistance
- Proactive campaigns and inactive-user recovery
- Internal workflows like operations support, HR, or knowledge management

## Getting Started

To start creating your first AI agent, go to the Agents section in the console and select "Create New Agent." From there, you'll follow the initial setup flow for identity, knowledge, and capabilities.

For more detailed instructions, explore the documentation sections for each specific feature of the Semantiks AI platform.
